## Source: Daniel Priestley — Key Person of Influence + Oversubscribed
Darius is currently in the **Dent Accelerator** programme.

### KPI 5-Step Method (Key Person of Influence)
Position Darius/InterioApp as the go-to authority in window furnishing e-commerce.

1. **Pitch** — A clear, compelling one-liner
   - *Current:* "Pricing technology for window furnishing retailers"
   - *Refined:* "We turn window furnishing showrooms into 24/7 online stores — accurate pricing, instant quotes, no spreadsheets"

2. **Publish** — Share expertise to build authority
   - YouTube channel: @InteriorShopApps (created, needs content)
   - Instagram: @interiorshopapps (created, needs first post)
   - Blog on website (to be added)
   - **Content themes:** Industry pain points, behind-the-scenes app building, retailer success stories, pricing strategy tips

3. **Productise** — Package expertise into scalable offerings
   - **Tier 1:** Shopify pricing calculator app (self-serve, £29-99/mo)
   - **Tier 2:** Full Shopify store build + app (done-for-you, £2,000-5,000)
   - **Tier 3:** Strategy consulting for scaling retailers (£5,000+)
   - **Free entry:** Waitlist + lead magnet (e.g., "The Window Furnishing E-commerce Checklist")

4. **Profile** — Build visible online presence
   - Website ✅ (interiorshopapps.com)
   - Shopify App Store listing (once MVP is ready)
   - Guest appearances on industry podcasts
   - LinkedIn content from Darius as founder

5. **Partnerships** — Strategic alliances
   - Fabric/blind suppliers who refer retailers
   - Shopify agencies who don't specialise in made-to-measure
   - Interior design communities and trade shows

### Oversubscribed Framework
Create more demand than capacity:
- **Signal demand before building** — Waitlist ✅ (already on website)
- **Create scarcity** — Limited launch cohort ("First 50 retailers get founder pricing")
- **Market before you sell** — Content, social proof, case studies
- **7-11-4 rule** — People need 7 hours of content, 11 touchpoints, across 4 locations before buying

## Source: Steven Bartlett — The 33 Laws (Diary of a CEO)
Key laws applicable to InterioApp:

### Relevant Laws for InterioApp
- **Fill your five buckets in the right order** — Knowledge → Skills → Network → Resources → Reputation
  - *Applied:* Darius has the industry knowledge. Now building skills (tech), network (Dent), resources (app), reputation (brand)

- **You must lean in to bizarre behaviour** — Stand out, don't blend in
  - *Applied:* An AI-powered calculator for window furnishings IS bizarre/niche. Lean into it. Own the weird specificity.

- **You do not get to choose what you believe** — Your environment shapes beliefs
  - *Applied:* Being in the Dent Accelerator is the right environment for growth mindset

- **You must sweat the small stuff** — Excellence in detail builds trust
  - *Applied:* The website design, app UX, onboarding flow — every detail matters for merchant trust

- **The first rule of the Diary of a CEO** — "You are the CEO of your own life"
  - *Applied:* Darius is building InterioApp as a CEO, not an employee. Brand = Darius + the company.
