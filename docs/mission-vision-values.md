# InteriorShopApps — Mission, Vision & Values

## Mission
To empower curtain, blind, and shutter retailers worldwide to sell made-to-measure products online — effortlessly, accurately, and beautifully.

## Vision
To become the #1 Shopify app ecosystem for the window furnishing industry, trusted by 10,000+ retailers across the US, UK, Australia, New Zealand, Canada, and Europe. We envision a future where every curtain and blind retailer — from a one-person shop to a national chain — can run a world-class online store with AI-powered tools that rival enterprise solutions.

## Values

### 1. Craft Over Shortcuts
Every pixel, every calculation, every interaction is designed with intention. We don't ship "good enough." We ship work we're proud of — because our merchants' customers will see it.

### 2. No Problem
No money? We find a way. No tools? We build them. No experience? We learn fast. Obstacles are just the next thing to solve.

### 3. Industry First
We are not a generic calculator slapped onto Shopify. We understand fabric widths, pattern repeats, lining types, heading styles, and regional measurement standards. Our tools speak the language of the trade.

### 4. Radical Simplicity
Complex products, simple experience. A retailer in Birmingham and a manufacturer in Melbourne should both be able to set up and start selling in under an hour.

### 5. Merchant Success = Our Success
We don't just sell software — we help merchants build their businesses. Store setup, maintenance, advice, support. We're the digital handyman for brick-and-mortar shops going online.

### 6. Global by Default
Built for every market from day one. US inches, UK millimetres, Australian standards. Multiple currencies, tax rules, and regional pricing logic — baked in, not bolted on.

### 7. Transparent & Trustworthy
Open about our pricing, honest about what our tools can and can't do. We earn five-star reviews, we don't game them. Our merchants trust us with their business — we take that seriously.

## Target Clients

### Primary: B2B Retailers
Curtain and blind shops — both brick-and-mortar and online — who want to sell made-to-measure products through Shopify. From independent shops to multi-location chains.

### Secondary: Manufacturers
Made-to-measure blind, curtain, and shutter manufacturers who want to offer direct-to-consumer or trade portals via Shopify.

### Tertiary: Vendors & Suppliers
Fabric and blinds component suppliers who want to give their retail customers easy online ordering tools.
