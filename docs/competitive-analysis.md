## Key Gaps & Opportunities

### What's MISSING in the market:
1. **No AI-powered specialist app** — Generic calculators exist, but nothing uses AI for measurement validation, smart recommendations, or pricing optimization
2. **No multi-region quoting** — Nobody handles regional standards (AU/NZ vs UK vs US measurements, fabric widths, etc.)
3. **No integration ecosystem** — Our InterioApp quoting system integration is unique
4. **FLOWRiX isn't on Shopify** — The most comprehensive competitor isn't even competing on Shopify. Huge gap.
5. **No onboarding/services play** — Nobody offers "we'll set up your entire online store" as a service alongside the app
6. **Reviews are low across the board** — Even top apps have <10 reviews. First to 100 wins.
7. **No content marketing** — Nobody is seriously doing YouTube/Instagram for this niche
8. **Multi-product gap** — No single app handles blinds + curtains + shutters + wallpaper well on Shopify
9. **No voice/chat advisory support** — Everyone just has email support

### What merchants complain about:
- Setup complexity (need hand-holding)
- Language/localisation issues
- Generic tools don't understand curtain/blind industry specifics
- Want custom measurements per region
- Need better UX for end customers
