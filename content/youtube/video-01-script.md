---

### SECTION 2 — THE PROBLEM (0:10–1:15)

> *[Talking head with B-roll: screenshots of clunky product pages, abandoned cart stats]*

Selling custom window furnishings online is genuinely hard. Think about what a customer has to do:

- Pick a product — roller blind, venetian, curtain, shutter
- Choose a fabric or material
- Enter exact measurements — width, drop, maybe recess vs. face-fit
- Understand pricing that changes based on every single one of those variables

Now imagine you're a regular consumer trying to do that on a Shopify store. Most of the time, the experience is confusing. The pricing doesn't make sense. There's no guidance. And the customer does what any reasonable person would do — they leave.

Industry data shows cart abandonment rates for made-to-measure products can exceed **70%**. That's not a traffic problem. That's a UX problem.

---

### SECTION 3 — WHAT EXISTS TODAY (1:15–2:00)

> *[Screen recording: quick scroll through Shopify App Store search results]*

Now, there *are* apps on the Shopify App Store that try to solve this. Generic product calculators, custom pricing tools, a few quote builders.

Here's the issue:

- **They're generic.** They don't understand window furnishings. You end up spending weeks configuring formulas that still don't cover your full catalogue.
- **No AI.** No smart recommendations, no measurement guidance, nothing that helps the customer feel confident.
- **No multi-region support.** If you sell in the UK *and* Australia, you're dealing with different measurement standards, different pricing conventions, different tax rules — and most apps just don't handle that.
- **No industry logic.** Things like minimum order widths, fabric roll constraints, bracket deductions — these are standard in the trade, but invisible to generic tools.

So retailers end up duct-taping solutions together, or worse, just taking phone orders and losing the online channel entirely.

---

### SECTION 4 — WHAT WE'RE BUILDING (2:00–3:30)

> *[Talking head, then transition to screen recording / UI mockup walkthrough]*

That's why we're building **InterioApp** — a Shopify app designed from the ground up for the curtain, blind, and shutter industry.

Here's what it does:

**🧠 AI-Powered Calculator**
Not just a formula engine. An intelligent calculator that *understands* window furnishings. It knows that a roller blind has different pricing logic to a wave-heading curtain. It can suggest the right product based on window type. It guides the customer through measurements step-by-step.

**🌍 Regional Pricing Standards**
Sell in multiple countries? InterioApp handles regional measurement units, local pricing conventions, currency, and tax rules — out of the box. UK, US, Australia, New Zealand, Canada, EU — all supported.

**⚡ One-Click Quoting**
Your customer configures their product, sees an instant price, and checks out. No back-and-forth emails. No "request a quote" dead ends. A real, complete e-commerce experience for made-to-measure products.

**🛠 Expert Support**
We're not a generic SaaS company that happens to sell to retailers. We understand this industry. Our support team knows the difference between a face-fit and a recess-fit, and our onboarding is built for window furnishing businesses specifically.

> *[Back to talking head]*

The goal is simple: give your customers the confidence to buy custom window furnishings online — and give you the tools to sell them at scale.
