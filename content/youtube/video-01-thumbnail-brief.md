# Video 01 — Thumbnail Brief

## Text Overlay
- Primary: **"Shopify + Blinds?"** (large, bold, white with dark shadow)
- Secondary: **"Finally. An app that gets it."** (smaller, below primary, light yellow or white)

## Visual Composition
- **Left third:** Presenter face, slightly surprised/intrigued expression, looking toward text
- **Right two-thirds:** Text overlay on a background showing a stylish room with curtains/blinds (slightly blurred)
- **Bottom-right corner:** Small Shopify bag icon or logo (subtle, not dominant)

## Colour Palette
- Background tones: deep teal or navy (#0B3D4C or #1A1A2E)
- Text: white (#FFFFFF) with a warm accent colour — amber/gold (#F5A623) for emphasis words
- High contrast throughout — must be legible at mobile thumbnail size

## Style
- Clean, modern, not cluttered
- No more than 6–8 words visible
- Faces perform well — include presenter if possible
- Avoid stock-photo feel; aim for authentic + professional

## Reference Feel
Think: tech startup announcement meets home interiors magazine. Confident, not salesy.

## File Dimensions
- 1280 × 720 px (16:9)
- Safe zone: keep key text/face within centre 80%
